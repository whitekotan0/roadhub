**Roadmap Hub** is a lightweight, open-source platform for creating, sharing, and tracking educational roadmaps. 

Unlike traditional linear lists, this tool visualizes knowledge as a **Skill Tree** (similar to video games). It helps learners understand dependencies, track their progress, and see the "big picture" of any subjectâ€”from Mathematics to Knitting.

It is **Serverless**. Everything runs in the browser. You own your data (JSON).
